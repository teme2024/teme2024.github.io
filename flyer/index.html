Q: How can I travel to IU Bloomington?
A: Visitors traveling on commercial airlines should fly into the Indianapolis International Airport. Those traveling on private planes may use the Monroe County Airport. The following shuttle companies provide transportation from the Indianapolis International Airport:
  • GO Express Travel: 3200 W VENTURE BLVD BLOOMINGTON IN 47404, Phone: +1-800-589-6004
  • Classic Touch Limousine: 2620 N WALNUT ST #1210 BLOOMINGTON IN 47404, Phone: +1-812-339-7269
Greyhound also provides bus service from Indianapolis International Airport Bus Station to Bloomington Bus Station (0.7 Mile, 14 Minute Walk To Biddle Hotel).
Q: What are the parking options on campus?
A: There are multiple parking options:
  • If staying at the Indiana University Memorial, Biddle Hotel & Conference Center, you'll receive a complimentary parking pass upon check-in.
  • For off-campus visitors, we recommend parking in the Henderson Parking Garage.
  • On weekdays, you can exchange the gate ticket for a $10 one-day visitor EM-P permit at the Office of Parking Operations in the lower level of the garage.
  • Parking is free from 6 p.m. on Friday through 7 a.m. on Monday, unless otherwise noted.
  • The garage accepts American Express, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, or Visa.
Office of Parking Operations: Henderson Parking Garage, 310 S FESS AVE BLOOMINGTON IN 47401-5097, Phone: +1-812-844-9848
